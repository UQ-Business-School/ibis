Timeline
Description

Used to illustrate a time-series or process to guide the learner through a linear path.

Using a Timeline template, you can add in dates or ordered numbering for each of the elements within your path.

Simple to edit and modify. Changes are applied automatically - no downloading or saving required. Just make your edits and hit refresh!

Supports / Features
  • Text, images, videos, audio recordings, screen recordings or drawings
  • Embed file attachments
  • Links to other websites or other Padlets
  • Insert YouTube, Spotify or Google Maps into presentation
  • Embed polls, AI images or AI Speech
Important Instructions
  • Click on the "Create new" to duplicate the template.
  • Modify your Padlet as needed.
When ready to publish, click on:
  1. Share panel
  2. Share
  3. Embed in your blog or website
  4. Scroll down to Embed Slideshow
  5. Change embed size to large
  6. Click Copy slideshow embed code
  7. Paste it as an HTML block as needed in Blackboard Ultra
